Gingerbread Cookies

A soft, spiced cookie perfect for eating while bundled up, watching the snow fall.

  • 3/4 Cup Shortening
  • 1 Cup Sugar
  • 1 Egg
  • 1/2 Cup Minus 1 Tablespoon (7 Tablespoons) Molasses
  • 2 1/3 Cup Flour
  • 2 Teaspoons Baking Soda
  • 1 Teaspoon Cinnamon
  • 1 Teaspoon Ginger
  • 1 Teaspoon Cloves
  1. Mix together shortening, sugar and egg.
  2. Add Molasses, Flour, baking soda and spices.
  3. Chill completely.
  4. Roll into a 2 inch log, cut into 1/4 inch slices and place onto a greased baking sheet.
  5. Bake in a 375Â° oven for 15 minutes.
